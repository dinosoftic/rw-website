Signs You Have Iron in Your Water

Iron is one of the most common water quality issues in Northern Minnesota. Here's how to tell if you have an iron problem:

  • Orange or rust-colored stains on fixtures, toilets, and sinks
  • Reddish-brown discoloration in your water
  • Metallic taste in your drinking water
  • Stained laundry with orange or brown spots
  • Clogged pipes and reduced water pressure
  • Rotten egg smell (indicates sulfur along with iron)

How Iron Removal Works

Our iron filtration systems use advanced oxidation and filtration technology to remove iron, manganese, and hydrogen sulfide from your water.

Benefits of Iron Removal:

  • No more rust stains on fixtures and appliances
  • Clean, bright laundry without discoloration
  • Better tasting water without metallic flavor
  • No more rotten egg smell
  • Protected plumbing and appliances
  • Crystal clear water from every tap

Why Chemical-Free Iron Removal Matters

Many iron removal systems on the market rely on chemicals like potassium permanganate or chlorine injection to oxidize iron. While these methods work, they come with drawbacks: you need to regularly purchase and handle chemicals, they can leave a chemical taste or smell in your water, and they add ongoing maintenance costs.

Hellenbrand's Iron Curtain technology takes a different approach. By using compressed air to oxidize dissolved iron, the system converts it into a solid particle that's easily trapped by the filter media. The result is iron-free water without any chemical additives—safer for your family, easier to maintain, and more cost-effective over the life of the system.

Our technicians have installed hundreds of Iron Curtain systems across the Iron Range, Brainerd Lakes, and the greater Duluth area.
