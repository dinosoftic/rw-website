Why Whole House RO?

While point-of-use RO systems only purify water at one location, whole house reverse osmosis treats ALL the water in your home. This is the ultimate solution for:

  • Homes with severely contaminated well water
  • High TDS (total dissolved solids) water
  • Areas with arsenic or other dangerous contaminants
  • Families who want the purest water possible
  • Sensitive individuals with health concerns

Benefits of Whole House RO

  • Pure water from every tap in your home
  • Removes up to 99% of all contaminants
  • Healthier showers and baths
  • Better for sensitive skin conditions
  • Protects all appliances and plumbing
  • No more bottled water needed
  • Peace of mind for your family's health

Our Recommended Whole House RO Systems

We carry two proven whole house reverse osmosis platforms from Hellenbrand: the N Series and the HRO 3.5. Both are purpose-built to deliver RO-quality water to every faucet, shower, and appliance in your home—not just the kitchen sink.

Hellenbrand N Series Whole House RO

The N Series is Hellenbrand's next-generation whole house membrane system, engineered for outstanding efficiency and high flow rates. Its advanced membrane technology produces more purified water with less waste, making it both eco-friendly and economical. The N Series is ideal for households that want whole-house RO purification with a compact footprint and simplified maintenance.

  • Advanced high-efficiency membrane technology
  • Higher flow rate with reduced water waste
  • Compact design for easier installation
  • Easy filter replacement with twist-off cartridges
  • Delivers purified water to every tap in your home

Hellenbrand HRO 3.5

The HRO 3.5 is Hellenbrand's high-capacity whole house RO system, built for homes that demand maximum purification volume. With commercial-grade TFC membranes capable of producing up to 3,500 gallons per day, it removes up to 99% of dissolved contaminants—including arsenic, lead, nitrates, fluoride, and total dissolved solids. A pressurized storage tank and variable-speed repressurization pump deliver consistent water pressure throughout your entire home.

  • Up to 3,500 GPD production capacity
  • Removes up to 99% of dissolved contaminants
  • Commercial-grade TFC membranes
  • Pressurized storage tank with repressurization pump
  • Designed for large homes and high water demand

Custom Whole House RO Solutions

Every home's water is different. For properties with complex water chemistry—extremely high TDS, multiple contaminants, or unique flow requirements—we design custom whole house RO systems tailored to your specific water test results. Our technicians combine Hellenbrand components with pre-treatment stages like softening and iron removal to create a complete, integrated purification system that delivers consistently pure water from every tap.

  • Custom-designed around your water test results
  • Pre-treatment stages for complex water chemistry
  • Integrated softening + RO for complete purification
  • Sized to your household's flow rate and demand
  • Professional installation with ongoing local service

Why the HRO 3.5 Is the Gold Standard for Whole House RO

Most RO systems are designed for a single faucet. The Hellenbrand HRO 3.5 is engineered from the ground up to purify water for your entire home. With a production capacity of up to 3,500 gallons per day, it can handle the demands of large families, multiple bathrooms, and water-intensive appliances—all while maintaining the water quality you'd expect from a top-tier RO system.

The HRO 3.5 is backed by Hellenbrand's factory warranty and designed for professional installation. Our technicians size the system based on your home's water test results, daily usage, and flow requirements, ensuring it performs flawlessly from day one. We also provide ongoing maintenance and membrane replacement services so your system continues to deliver pure water year after year.

If you have severely contaminated well water, high TDS levels, arsenic, or other dangerous contaminants, the HRO 3.5 is the system that gives you peace of mind. It's the closest thing to bottled water quality from every tap in your house.
