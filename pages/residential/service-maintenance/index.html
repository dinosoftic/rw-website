Our Services

Routine Maintenance

Every water treatment system needs periodic maintenance to perform at its best. For Hellenbrand ProMate 6 and DMT softeners, that means checking the resin bed, inspecting the control valve, verifying regeneration cycles, and ensuring salt levels are optimal. For Hellenbrand Millennium Pro and N Series RO systems, we replace sediment filters, carbon pre-filters, and post-filters on a recommended schedule—and test membrane performance to make sure you're still getting 99%+ contaminant reduction. Iron Curtain systems get a media bed inspection and backwash cycle verification.

  • ProMate softener resin and valve inspections
  • RO filter and membrane replacement (Millennium Pro, N Series)
  • Iron Curtain media bed and backwash checks
  • HRO 3.5 membrane performance testing
  • Water quality testing at every visit

Repairs & Troubleshooting

When something goes wrong with your water system, our factory-trained technicians diagnose and fix it fast. We carry common Hellenbrand replacement parts on our service trucks—control valve components, motors, seals, circuit boards, and filter cartridges—so most repairs are completed in a single visit. Whether your ProMate softener isn't regenerating properly, your Millennium Pro RO has low pressure, or your Iron Curtain Jr isn't clearing iron like it used to, we've seen it before and we know how to fix it.

  • Hellenbrand control valve and motor repairs
  • RO membrane and pressure troubleshooting
  • Iron Curtain air-injection system diagnostics
  • Leak detection and plumbing repairs
  • Most repairs completed in a single visit

System Upgrades

If your current water system is more than 15 years old, it's likely using outdated technology that wastes salt, water, and energy. Upgrading to a modern Hellenbrand ProMate 6 softener with demand-initiated regeneration can cut your salt usage significantly. Replacing an old RO system with a Hellenbrand Millennium Pro or N Series gives you better water quality with easier filter changes. We'll assess your current setup, test your water, and recommend the most cost-effective upgrade path.

  • Upgrade to Hellenbrand ProMate 6 or DMT Twin
  • Replace old RO with Millennium Pro or N Series
  • Add Iron Curtain Jr to existing softener setup
  • Free water test and upgrade consultation
  • Trade-in credit on qualifying old systems
