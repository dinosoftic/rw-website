Commercial Water Treatment Solutions

We use the same proven Hellenbrand and 3M equipment trusted in residential applications—scaled up for commercial demand. Every system is sized and configured specifically for your business's water usage, flow requirements, and water quality challenges.

Commercial Water Softeners

Our commercial softener installations use Hellenbrand's DMT Twin Alternating systems, which provide continuous soft water even during regeneration—critical for businesses that can't afford a moment of hard water. The twin-tank design means one tank is always online while the other regenerates, so your restaurant's dishwasher, hotel's laundry, or manufacturing line never sees hard water scale. We size the system based on your peak flow rates and daily water usage to ensure reliable performance under commercial demand.

  • Hellenbrand DMT Twin Alternating for 24/7 operation
  • Sized for commercial flow rates and peak demand
  • Eliminates scale in boilers, steamers, and dishwashers
  • Metered regeneration maximizes salt efficiency
  • Reduces soap, detergent, and chemical costs

Commercial Reverse Osmosis

For businesses that need ultra-pure water, our commercial RO systems deliver. Built around Hellenbrand's high-capacity membrane technology, these systems produce hundreds to thousands of gallons of purified water daily. They're ideal for restaurants that need spot-free glassware, breweries that require precise water chemistry, healthcare facilities with strict water standards, and any commercial operation where water purity directly impacts product quality or equipment longevity.

  • High-capacity Hellenbrand membrane technology
  • Hundreds to thousands of GPD production
  • Critical for food service, brewing, and healthcare
  • Reduces TDS for consistent product quality
  • Pre-treatment included to protect membranes

Commercial Filtration & Iron Removal

Northern Minnesota businesses on well water often face the same iron, manganese, and sediment issues as residential customers—just at a larger scale. Our commercial filtration solutions use Hellenbrand's ProMate 6 Filter platform and Iron Curtain technology, configured for higher flow rates and continuous operation. We design systems for laundromats dealing with iron staining, resorts with sediment-heavy well water, car washes that need spot-free rinse water, and more.

  • Hellenbrand ProMate 6 Filter for commercial volume
  • Iron Curtain chemical-free iron removal
  • Custom multi-stage configurations available
  • Automatic backwash for low-maintenance operation
  • Designed for continuous commercial demand

The Business Case for Professional Water Treatment

Poor water quality silently drains your bottom line. Scale buildup in a commercial dishwasher reduces heating efficiency by up to 30%, costing you hundreds in extra energy per year. Iron-stained linens at a hotel drive negative reviews. Hard water deposits in a commercial espresso machine can lead to a $2,000 repair bill. And for breweries or food manufacturers, inconsistent water chemistry directly impacts product taste and consistency.

Investing in a properly sized commercial water treatment system pays for itself through reduced equipment repairs, lower energy costs, fewer chemical purchases, and improved product quality. Our team performs a thorough water analysis of your business, evaluates your equipment and usage, and designs a treatment system that addresses your specific challenges.

  • Reduced equipment maintenance—fewer breakdowns and repairs
  • Extended equipment lifespan saves thousands in replacement costs
  • Improved product consistency for food, beverage, and manufacturing
  • Spot-free glassware and cleaner linens without extra chemicals
  • Lower energy costs from scale-free heating elements
  • Compliance with health and safety water regulations
