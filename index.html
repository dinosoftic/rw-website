Chemical-Free Iron Removal with the Hellenbrand Iron Curtain

Orange stains, metallic taste, and rotten egg smell are signs of high iron in your well water—a common problem across the Iron Range and Northern Minnesota. Our Hellenbrand Iron Curtain Jr uses patented air-injection oxidation to remove iron, manganese, and hydrogen sulfide without any chemicals. Pair it with a ProMate 6 softener and you've solved both iron and hardness in one system.

  • Hellenbrand Iron Curtain Jr—no chemicals needed
  • Removes iron, manganese, and hydrogen sulfide
  • Pairs with ProMate 6 for complete treatment
